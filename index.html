We're creating a platform that helps artists connect with their audiences.

Created by producers and theatre makers, Scottie helps you easily create beautiful theatre websites that help you connect to your audience and sell more tickets. We're a platform not an agency and we want to grow with you from first performances to world-renowned stages.

Easy to create and update

You shouldn't have to worry about the security headaches that come with hosting your own site. Use Scottie's intuitive drag and drop website builder, powered by your existing data, secured and updated by us.

Box office integration

Stop entering the same information into multiple systems, let Scottie help you work smarter not harder. Stop your customers being sent across the web when they try to buy your tickets.

Your customers, your data

Build deep and lasting relationships with your fans, crowd-funding supporters and ticket buyers and keep track of them in one place - Scottie.

Your home on the Internet

Tell your story using the channels you know and have invested in like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram, creating a curated home on the web for your work.
